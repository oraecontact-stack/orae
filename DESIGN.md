# Design Brief – ORAE Savon Liquide

## Direction
Premium modern African brand. Bold Poppins typography, clean white breathing room, strong color contrast. Conversion-focused mobile-first minimal distraction.

## Tone
Local, trustworthy, efficient. "Orae, pour tout ce qui compte." Direct action orientation for families and small businesses in Lomé.

## Differentiation
Local Togolese positioning, Poppins Bold commanding typography, midnight blue + orange brand palette, WhatsApp integration, price transparency.

## Color Palette

| Role | OKLCH | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | `0.98 0 0` | #FFFFFF | Main canvas, clean space |
| Primary (Orange) | `0.65 0.22 50` | #FF6B00 | CTAs, buttons, strong accents |
| Text (Midnight Blue) | `0.28 0.09 260` | #1A2B5F | All body text, headings |
| Muted | `0.95 0 0` | #F8F8F8 | Subtle surfaces, dividers |

## Typography

| Layer | Font | Weight | Usage |
|-------|------|--------|-------|
| Display | Poppins | 700–800 | h1–h6 titles, hero headlines |
| Body | Inter | 400–600 | Copy, CTAs, descriptions |
| Mono | System | 400 | Prices, technical data |

## Elevation & Depth
Soft shadows (elevated: 0 20px 60px rgba(0,0,0,0.15); card: 0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)). Flat cards with gentle shadows, no clutter.

## Structural Zones

| Zone | Height | Content |
|------|--------|----------|
| Header | Fixed | Logo left, Commander button right |
| Hero | 60–70% viewport | Text left (Poppins Bold), product image right (rounded square) |
| Products | 100% viewport | 2–5 product cards, packs section |
| Eco-Responsible | Full width | Text + 4 pillar cards |
| Testimonials | 100% viewport | 5 social-proof cards |
| CTA Final | 40% viewport | Orange WhatsApp button, email contact |

## Spacing & Rhythm
Mobile-first: 1rem base rhythm. Touch targets: 44px minimum. Cards: 1.5rem padding. Section gaps: 2–3rem. Ample white space prioritized.

## Component Patterns
- **Buttons**: Orange (#FF6B00) bg, white text, 44px height, rounded-lg.
- **Cards**: White bg, soft shadow, rounded-lg, 1.5rem padding, midnight blue headings.
- **Text**: All in midnight blue #1A2B5F. Poppins Bold h1–h6. Inter regular body.

## Motion
Smooth transitions: 0.3s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1). Hover: opacity fade, scale micro. No auto-play. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.

## Constraints
- Mobile-first (min 320px width).
- All values in OKLCH or semantic tokens only.
- Poppins Bold from Google Fonts.
- Midnight blue + orange palette only.

## Signature Detail
Orange #FF6B00 reserved exclusively for CTAs and strong accents. Midnight blue #1A2B5F grounds all text. Poppins Bold commands hierarchy. White space dominates. Every decision serves WhatsApp conversion.
